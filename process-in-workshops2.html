Predestination Paradox
While doing an experiment in a lab, a fire breaks out, you manage to escape but your friend dies. Grieving, you invent a time machine and go back in time to prevent the fire from starting and saving your friend, but when you land you accidentally knock off a bottle full of oil while the burners are on, and you start the fire which caused your friend to die.
